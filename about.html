About Pinnacle Point Mining

Welcome to Pinnacle Point Mining, a dynamic and proudly black-owned company specializing in the construction and supply of lighting towers. Established with a vision for excellence, we focus on providing tailored solutions to open-cast mines seeking reliable and efficient lighting options for hire.

As a young and ambitious entity, Pinnacle Point Mining aims to illuminate the mining sector with cutting-edge technology and unparalleled service. Our commitment is rooted in building lighting towers that not only meet but exceed the demands of the industry. From construction to supplying, our offerings are designed to enhance visibility, safety, and overall operational efficiency within open-cast mines.

Business overview

Our company offers lighting plants at a competitive daily rate and this includes the servicing of the units on a monthly basis. Our employees are available after hours for any break downs or call outs. We comply with all the mines safety requirements and regulations like being subjected to medicals and also maintaining safety files for our lighting plants.

Mission Statement

Successfully deliver mining services to all mines in the most cost efficient manner and with zero LTI’s and a good safety record.

Vision

The vision of Pinnacle Point Mining is to be one of the leading and successful mining contracting BEE organizations, through providing safe and efficient service with proper management of funds, honest and excellent client service.
