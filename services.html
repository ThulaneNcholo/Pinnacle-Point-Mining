Lighting Plant hire service

Providing lighting plants for rental to mines and construction sites at a competitive rate.

Visuals of the units

These units are currently available in bright blue and grey colours. The trailer has been powder coated for the unit to withstand tough mine conditions. They are easy to move around as you can simply attach to your tow bar and move to any area where operations requires it.

Roadworthy Mobility

Ensure secure transportation with our licensed trailers, guaranteeing roadworthiness and reliable mobility for our lighting towers.

Customizable Illumination

Tailor your lighting needs with four options of 200/300/500 watts LED lights, providing flexibility based on the specific requirements of your mining site.

Versatile Vertical Control

Adapt to varying terrains effortlessly with our lighting towers' adjustable mast, reaching a maximum height of 4 meters. The manual control ensures precise and versatile vertical positioning.

Safety First with Mounted Fire Protection

Prioritize safety on-site with our mounted 9Kg fire extinguisher, readily available to address any unforeseen emergencies, ensuring a secure working environment.

Powerful Yanmar Generator

Experience peak performance with our Diesel generator (4 to 6 KVA). The Yanmar Generator, recognized as the epitome of generators in the market, ensures a reliable and constant power supply for your operations.

Safety Precautions at Your Fingertips

Incorporate an additional layer of safety with our isolation switch, providing a quick and efficient means to secure the lighting tower, enhancing overall safety precautions at the mining site.
